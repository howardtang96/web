About Us

Muchacho Trading Company is a trading company (buying agent) assisting South America Countries (especially Argentina/Brasil and Mexico) importer/supplier to fulfil what their souring in ASIA. Our company established since Feb 2023 in Hong Kong. We started running Muchacho Trading because we have great connections with factories and Freight Forwarder in China and Hong Kong.

In the first part, Muchacho is a common spanish word stand for a young man. The idea come from an Argentina song called :"Muchachos, Ahora nos volvimos a ilusionar" singing about La Scaloneta in world cup 2022. We hope to be your guys and guides to provide the best deal and services for you all.

In the second part, Trading is referring connections/bridge. We hope we could connect China/Hong Kong and Latin America as much as we can. We would like to support both import/export side business as we can!
